The Empathy
Pipeline

How do we make an AI feel empathetic? Standard LLMs are cold and robotic. We split the task into two distinct neural networks, working together seamlessly to first understand the human, and then respond to them.

1

Emotion Detection

We use a fine-tuned DistilRoBERTa model to instantly analyze the customer's text and classify their underlying emotion (Anger, Joy, Fear, etc.).

2

Tone Conditioning

The detected emotion is combined with your brand's preferred tone (Friendly, Formal, or Apologetic) to construct a highly specific contextual prompt.

3

Generative Response

Our LoRA fine-tuned Flan-T5 model generates the final response, synthesizing the customer's issue, their emotion, and your brand's tone perfectly.

System Architecture

By utilizing Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) with LoRA, we successfully adapted a lightweight foundation model to outperform models 10x its size on empathy-specific tasks.
